2017 Texas MWCS Symposium

Call for Student Posters

Deadline for Abstracts: March 21, 2017

One of the features of the 2016 Texas Symposium on Wireless and Microwave Circuits and Systems is a Student Poster Session, and both graduate and undergraduate students are welcome to submit to this. The poster session will allow the student presenters to present their posters and interact with the symposium attendees, including other students, faculty, and industry representatives. Awards will be given for the best poster presentations.

It is not necessary for students to have an oral presentation at the symposium in order to have a poster presentation. However, students who have an oral presentation at the symposium are also invited to have a poster presentation on the same topic if they wish, which will allow them to interact with the attendees more fully on their topic.

Student poster presenters are asked to submit a one-page abstract, which will be published in the conference booklet. Please use the template provided on this website for preparing the abstract, and email the completed abstract (as a Word document) to Prof. David R. Jackson at djackson@uh.edu by the deadline of 8:00 a.m. on Monday, March 21, 2016. Students are allowed to prepare their posters in any format that they wish. Either landscape or portrait mode is acceptable. Foam boards measuring 40 inches ´ 60 inches will be provided, along with tacks for fastening the posters to the foam boards. Easels that the posters will be placed on will also be provided. Students should bring their posters with them to the symposium and have their posters fastened to the foam boards and placed on the easels in the poster room in time for the start of the poster session.

Prizes will be awarded for the best student posters, so students are encouraged to submit to the Student Poster Session and to do their best!
